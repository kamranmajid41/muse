How-to-use

To generate text with Muse first thing you must do is pick a seed text, seed text might be anything a word, sentence, or a paragraph something you believe will make sense to AI after that you can press the generate button and see AI's prediction about your seed.

About Muse

Muse is an AI that uses deep learning to generate rap song lyrics you can see the live demo from your browser type a seed text and generate rap songs around 1000 characters.

Muse is built with

  • Python
  • TensorFlow & Keras
  • Flask
  • Bootstrap
